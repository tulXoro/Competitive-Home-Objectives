Competitive Home Objectives

Created by: Aaron Lim, Tim Lor, Edgarventura Melendrez, and Ethan Nelson

1. Add in the people who will be doing all of the busy work, then press start!
2. Set up a timer! Decide how long you want to play!

3. To claim a task, complete it before anyone else, then click on the task and type in your name!
4. The points will be added onto your score!
5. When the timer ends, the person with the highest score wins!
